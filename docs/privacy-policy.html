GeoLens — GPS Map Camera

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

GeoLens is a GPS watermark camera app built by an independent developer. This policy explains what data is accessed when you use the app and how it is handled.

Data We Access

  • Precise Location (GPS): Used to fetch your coordinates, street address, and matching satellite map tile. Location is processed entirely on-device and embedded into your photos. It is never uploaded to any server we control.
  • Camera: Used to capture photos. No video or audio is recorded.
  • Photos / Storage: Captured photos are saved to your device's local storage only. They are never uploaded or synced anywhere automatically.

Third-Party Services

To display the satellite map thumbnail, the app makes HTTPS requests to Esri World Imagery (server.arcgisonline.com). Your approximate GPS coordinates are included in the tile request URL so the correct map tile can be returned — no personal identifiers are attached. Please refer to Esri's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle these requests.

No analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or any other third-party SDK is included in the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or transmit your personal data — including location, photos, or any other information — to any third party.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data (photos, settings) lives entirely on your device. You can delete individual photos or batches from within the app's gallery at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all app data from your device.

Permissions Used

  • Camera — required to capture photos.
  • Location (Fine / GPS) — required to embed coordinates and fetch the matching satellite map tile.

Both permissions are requested at runtime. You may deny or revoke them at any time in your device's Settings. Denying location disables GPS watermarking; denying camera disables photo capture.

Children's Privacy

GeoLens is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
